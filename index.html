Our idea :

Our idea is to create a dance hall chain, where classes are given for all ages.

How to choose?

Choosing our room means choosing to exert yourself by practicing a sport that you love, but also to give free rein to your inspiration and to create or propose choreography yourself. It is then a good way to stimulate the brain of the youngest.

Raphael Ruffin (raphael.ruffin@ieseg.fr)

Why call on us?

Call us because this dance hall chain recruits from all levels, and create a large community where exchanges between different halls in different cities and countries are encouraged.
